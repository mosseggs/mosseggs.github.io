What is Hylan Boulevard?

Hylan Blvd is the 2nd longest street in New York City, running 14 miles from the top to bottom of Staten Island. It falls just behind Broadway, which has 13 Miles in Manhattan, 2 Miles in the Bronx, and 18 miles past New York City. Hylan is one of the staple locations of Staten Island, the others being Staten Island Mall and New Dorp. It's also home to shops from a variety of cultures, such as turkish, chinese and greek.


My Essay

For my AP Language and Composition class, we had to write an essay about how we viewed New York City, called "Write Your New York". For My Essay, I wrote about Hylan Boulevard, comparing it to SoHo in Manhattan and the differences between Staten Island and the rest of NYC.
