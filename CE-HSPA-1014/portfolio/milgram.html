Summary

      Milgram is an interactive multi-media project by created by DECO*27 and Yamanaka Takuya. The main series is based in a prison with 10 prisoners, and 2 guards. The guards are a child of unknown gender that appears to be around 15 named Es, and a Jackalope named Jackalope.

      Each of the prisoners have committed a crime that, in some sense, could be called murder. Acting through the eyes of Es, you must judge the prisoners as "forgivable" or "unforgivable" on each of three trials, after watching the VD(Voice Drama) and the Music Video for that trial. The VD showcases an interrogation taken just before the song, and showcases the prisoner's personality and mental state. The Song, on the other hand, is a window into the prisoner's mind. It cannot lie, but everything is hidden under a veil of symbolism. You can vote through the Milgram Website, or on the App, which also contains conversations between prisoners.
